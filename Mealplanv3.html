4-Week Vegan Meal Plan

Week 1

Breakfast: Vegan Protein Oats

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup oats
  • 1 tbsp chia seeds
  • 1 scoop vegan protein powder
  • 1/2 cup berries
  • 1 tbsp almond butter
  • 1 cup almond milk

Instructions: Cook oats with almond milk. Once cooked, stir in chia seeds, protein powder, and top with berries and almond butter.

Macros: Calories: ~500, Protein: ~30g, Fat: ~18g, Carbs: ~50g

Lunch: Chickpea & Quinoa Salad

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup cooked quinoa
  • 1/2 cup chickpeas
  • 1/4 cup cucumber (diced)
  • 1/4 cup tomato (diced)
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions: Toss cooked quinoa, chickpeas, cucumber, tomato, olive oil, lemon juice, salt, and pepper in a bowl.

Macros: Calories: ~450, Protein: ~20g, Fat: ~15g, Carbs: ~55g

Dinner: Vegan Lentil & Sweet Potato Curry

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup dried lentils
  • 2 medium sweet potatoes (cubed)
  • 1 can coconut milk
  • 1 medium onion (diced)
  • 2 garlic cloves (minced)
  • 1 tbsp curry powder
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 cup vegetable broth

Instructions: Sauté onion and garlic in olive oil. Add the sweet potatoes and spices, then cook for 5 minutes. Add lentils, coconut milk, and vegetable broth. Simmer until lentils are soft and sweet potatoes are tender.

Macros: Calories: ~600, Protein: ~25g, Fat: ~25g, Carbs: ~80g

Snack: Apple with Peanut Butter

Ingredients:

  • 1 medium apple
  • 2 tbsp peanut butter

Macros: Calories: ~300, Protein: ~8g, Fat: ~18g, Carbs: ~30g

Week 2

Breakfast: Tofu Scramble with Avocado

Ingredients:

  • 1/2 block tofu
  • 1/2 avocado
  • 1/4 cup diced bell peppers
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions: Sauté bell peppers in olive oil. Crumble tofu into the pan and cook with turmeric, salt, and pepper until golden. Serve with sliced avocado.

Macros: Calories: ~450, Protein: ~30g, Fat: ~30g, Carbs: ~20g

Lunch: Vegan Buddha Bowl

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup cooked quinoa
  • 1/2 cup roasted chickpeas
  • 1/2 cup roasted sweet potato
  • 1/2 avocado
  • 1 tbsp tahini
  • 1 tbsp lemon juice

Instructions: Roast chickpeas and sweet potato with olive oil and seasoning. Assemble in a bowl with quinoa, avocado, and drizzle with tahini and lemon juice.

Macros: Calories: ~550, Protein: ~20g, Fat: ~25g, Carbs: ~70g

Dinner: Tempeh Stir-fry with Broccoli & Brown Rice

Ingredients:

  • 1 block tempeh
  • 2 cups broccoli florets
  • 1 cup cooked brown rice
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp sesame oil
  • 1 tsp ginger (minced)

Instructions: Sauté tempeh until browned, add broccoli and ginger, and cook for 5 minutes. Add soy sauce and sesame oil, then serve over brown rice.

Macros: Calories: ~600, Protein: ~30g, Fat: ~25g, Carbs: ~60g

Snack: Almonds & Dried Fruit

Ingredients:

  • 1 oz almonds
  • 1/4 cup dried cranberries

Macros: Calories: ~300, Protein: ~8g, Fat: ~20g, Carbs: ~25g

Week 3

Breakfast: Protein Smoothie

Ingredients:

  • 1 scoop vegan protein powder
  • 1/2 banana
  • 1 tbsp almond butter
  • 1 cup spinach
  • 1 cup almond milk

Instructions: Blend all ingredients until smooth.

Macros: Calories: ~400, Protein: ~30g, Fat: ~18g, Carbs: ~35g

Week 4

Breakfast: Avocado Toast with Hemp Seeds

Ingredients:

  • 2 slices whole-grain bread
  • 1/2 avocado
  • 2 tbsp hemp seeds
  • 1 tsp olive oil

Instructions: Toast bread, spread avocado, drizzle with olive oil, and sprinkle with hemp seeds.

Macros: Calories: ~400, Protein: ~10g, Fat: ~30g, Carbs: ~35g
